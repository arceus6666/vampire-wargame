Rules

Objective:

The objective of the game is to anihilate all
the pieces of the opponent.

Rules:

During each turn, players determinate wich
pieces can be moved using a spinning wheel.
When they lose two pieces, they will be
granted a second spin per turn. After losing
another two, they will receive a third spin.

The pieces:

All pieces can move to an empty square or
attack an adjacent enemy piece during a
turn. Apart from having different statistics,
each piece has an extra power:

Lycanthropes can move two squares, instead
of one.

Vampires can drain life from an adjacent
piece, dealing less damage but healing
themselves in exchange.

Necromancer can summon a Zombie
anywhere in the board, and can attack at
two squares of distance, ignoring armor
value.
