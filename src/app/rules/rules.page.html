Vampire Wargame

Rules:

Objective:

The objective of the game is to anihilate all the pieces of the opponent.

The pieces:

All pieces can move to an empty square or attack an adjacent enemy piece during a turn. Apart from having different statistics, each piece has an extra power:
Lycanthropes can move two squares, instead of one.
Vampires can drain life from an adjacent piece, dealing less damage but healing themselves in exchange.
Necromancer can summon a Zombie anywhere in the board, and can attack at two squares of distance, ignoring armor value.
